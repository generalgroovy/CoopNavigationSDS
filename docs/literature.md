# Speech Dialog Systems Literature Atlas

Sorted primarily by fundamental component/aspect and secondarily by release year. Evaluation labels mark whether a work is directly relevant to human evaluation, automatic evaluation, or both.

## Foundations and Architectures

- **1977** [GUS, a frame-driven dialog system](https://doi.org/10.1016/0004-3702(77)90018-2) - Bobrow, Kaplan, Kay, Norman, Thompson, Winograd. Artificial Intelligence. _system; human evaluation._ Early frame-based natural-language dialog architecture; useful baseline for slot/frame task dialogs before speech became practical.
- **1980** [Put-That-There: Voice and gesture at the graphics interface](https://doi.org/10.1145/800250.807503) - Bolt. SIGGRAPH. _system; human evaluation._ Canonical multimodal spoken interaction demo; established deixis, grounding, and multimodal reference as dialog-system concerns.
- **2000** [GALAXY-II: A reference architecture for conversational system development](https://groups.csail.mit.edu/sls/publications/2000/icslp00-galaxy.pdf) - Seneff, Hurley, Lau, Pao, Schmid, Zue. ICSLP. _architecture; automatic, human evaluation._ Hub-and-server architecture that shaped reusable SDS component integration.
- **2002** [VoiceXML 2.0](https://www.w3.org/TR/voicexml20/) - W3C Voice Browser Working Group. W3C Recommendation. _standard; automatic evaluation._ Industrial standard for telephony dialog applications, grammars, prompts, events, and form-filling flows.
- **2008** [A statistical approach to spoken dialog systems design and evaluation](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.specom.2008.04.005) - Griol, Hurtado, Segarra, Sanchis. Speech Communication. _system; automatic, human evaluation._ Uses a module pipeline and statistical dialog strategies for spontaneous speech information access.
- **2014** [Review of spoken dialogue systems](https://doi.org/10.3989/loquens.2014.012) - Lopez-Cozar, Callejas, Griol, Quesada. Loquens. _survey; human, automatic evaluation._ Readable survey of ASR, NLU, dialog management, NLG, speech synthesis, applications, and evolution.
- **2024** [A survey on recent advances in LLM-based multi-turn dialogue systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.18013) - Yi, Ouyang, Liu, Liao, Xu, Shen. arXiv. _survey; human, automatic evaluation._ Recent map of LLM adaptation, open-domain and task-oriented multi-turn dialog, datasets, and metrics.
## ASR and Speech Input

- **1989** [A tutorial on hidden Markov models and selected applications in speech recognition](https://doi.org/10.1109/5.18626) - Rabiner. Proceedings of the IEEE. _method; automatic evaluation._ Core statistical ASR foundation behind early SDS speech input and WER-centered component evaluation.
- **2011** [The Kaldi speech recognition toolkit](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/6700480) - Povey et al.. ASRU. _toolkit; automatic evaluation._ Open ASR toolkit that standardized reproducible acoustic-model experiments for speech interfaces.
- **2017** [Turn-taking and endpointing in spoken interaction](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.csl.2017.07.002) - Skantze. Computer Speech & Language. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Shows why SDS input evaluation must include timing, interruptions, and conversational floor management, not only WER.
- **2020** [wav2vec 2.0: A framework for self-supervised learning of speech representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.11477) - Baevski, Zhou, Mohamed, Auli. NeurIPS. _method; automatic evaluation._ Self-supervised speech representation learning shifted ASR evaluation and low-resource spoken-dialog feasibility.
- **2022** [Robust speech recognition via large-scale weak supervision](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.04356) - Radford et al.. ICML. _model; automatic evaluation._ Large-scale ASR model that changed practical SDS baselines for multilingual and noisy speech input.
## NLU and State Tracking

- **1991** [The ATIS spoken language systems benchmark for language understanding](https://aclanthology.org/H91-1023/) - DARPA SLS community. DARPA Speech and Natural Language Workshop. _benchmark; automatic evaluation._ Established query-answer and semantic interpretation evaluation for spoken language understanding.
## Dialog Management

- **1995** [The TRAINS project: A case study in building a conversational planning agent](https://doi.org/10.1080/09528139508953764) - Allen, Schubert, Ferguson, Heeman, Hwang, Kato, Light, Martin, Miller, Poesio, Traum. Journal of Experimental and Theoretical AI. _system; human evaluation._ Influential mixed-initiative planning dialog system; important for discourse state, intentions, and collaborative problem solving.
- **1999** [Speech acts for dialogue agents](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/221019611_Speech_Acts_for_Dialogue_Agents) - Traum. Foundations of Rational Agency. _theory; human evaluation._ Connects dialog acts, commitments, and agent behavior; still relevant for annotation schemes and policy design.
- **2013** [POMDP-based statistical spoken dialog systems: A review](https://doi.org/10.1109/JPROC.2012.2225812) - Young, Schatzmann, Weilhammer, Ye. Proceedings of the IEEE. _method; automatic, human evaluation._ Belief-state and uncertainty-aware dialog management became a central response to ASR/SLU errors.
- **2016** [End-to-end trainable task-oriented dialog systems](https://aclanthology.org/E17-1042/) - Wen, Vandyke, Mrksic, Gasic, Rojas-Barahona, Su, Ultes, Young. EACL. _method; automatic evaluation._ Neural end-to-end task-oriented architecture with benchmarked belief tracking and response generation.
## Response Generation

- **2005** [Evaluating natural language generation systems](https://doi.org/10.1162/089120105774321091) - Reiter and Belz. Computational Linguistics. _metric; human, automatic evaluation._ Foundational NLG evaluation guidance relevant to SDS prompt generation and response realization.
- **2015** [A neural conversational model](https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.05869) - Vinyals and Le. ICML Deep Learning Workshop. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Early sequence-to-sequence dialog generation; intensified concern about weak automatic text metrics.
- **2015** [Semantically conditioned LSTM-based natural language generation for spoken dialogue systems](https://aclanthology.org/D15-1199/) - Wen, Gasic, Mrksic, Su, Vandyke, Young. EMNLP. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Representative neural NLG for task-oriented SDS with slot-error and human naturalness evaluation.
## Speech Output and Prosody

- **2016** [WaveNet: A generative model for raw audio](https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.03499) - van den Oord et al.. arXiv / DeepMind. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Neural waveform generation that changed speech-output naturalness expectations and MOS evaluation.
- **2017** [Tacotron: Towards end-to-end speech synthesis](https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.10135) - Wang et al.. Interspeech. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ End-to-end text-to-speech architecture relevant to SDS response rendering, prosody, and perceived naturalness.
- **2023** [Neural codec language models are zero-shot text to speech synthesizers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.02111) - Wang et al.. arXiv. _model; human, automatic evaluation._ Codec-language-model TTS raises new spoken-dialog evaluation questions around speaker similarity, prosody, consent, and safety.
## End-to-End Spoken Models

- **2023** [SpeechGPT: Empowering large language models with intrinsic cross-modal conversational abilities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.11000) - Zhang et al.. arXiv. _system; human, automatic evaluation._ Representative speech-language LLM architecture using discrete speech units for spoken interaction.
- **2024** [GPT-4o system card](https://openai.com/index/gpt-4o-system-card/) - OpenAI. OpenAI. _system; human, automatic evaluation._ Industrial reference point for low-latency multimodal spoken interaction and safety evaluation.
- **2024** [Speech large language models: A survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.14668) - Speech LLM survey authors. arXiv. _survey; automatic, human evaluation._ Context for evaluating spoken LLMs across recognition, understanding, generation, and interaction.
- **2024** [WavChat: A survey of spoken dialogue models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13577) - Ji et al.. arXiv. _survey; human, automatic evaluation._ Surveys the shift from cascaded ASR-LLM-TTS systems to unified speech-to-speech dialog models, datasets, and evaluation metrics.
## Human Evaluation

- **1996** [Wizard-of-Oz prototyping for spoken dialogue systems](https://doi.org/10.1016/0950-7051(96)01036-7) - Dahlback, Jonsson, Ahrenberg. Knowledge-Based Systems. _method; human evaluation._ Practical methodology for collecting realistic dialogs and measuring user reactions before complete automation.
- **2000** [Subjective assessment of speech system interfaces (SASSI)](https://doi.org/10.1017/S1351324900002476) - Hone and Graham. Natural Language Engineering. _instrument; human evaluation._ Questionnaire instrument for user satisfaction, usability, cognitive demand, likeability, and annoyance.
- **2003** [ITU-T P.851: Subjective quality evaluation of telephone services based on spoken dialogue systems](https://www.itu.int/rec/T-REC-P.851/en) - ITU-T. Recommendation P.851. _standard; human evaluation._ Standardizes subjective evaluation setup, scenarios, user factors, questionnaires, and quality interpretation for telephone SDS.
- **2005** [Quality of telephone-based spoken dialogue systems](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/0-387-26188-X) - Moller. Springer. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Comprehensive quality model connecting perception, interaction parameters, and service-level evaluation.
- **2007** [Evaluating spoken dialogue systems according to de-facto standards: A case study](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.csl.2005.11.003) - Moller, Engelbrecht, Kuhnel, Wechsung, Weiss. Computer Speech & Language. _method; human, automatic evaluation._ Tests P.851, SASSI, interaction parameters, and PARADISE-style prediction on a domestic-control SDS.
- **2019** [Human evaluation of conversational agents: A review](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.05406) - See, Roller, Kiela, Weston. arXiv and dialog-evaluation literature. _survey; human evaluation._ Practical discussion of human-rating design, annotator variance, pairwise comparisons, and reporting.
- **2020** [FED: A framework and dataset for fine-grained evaluation of dialogue](https://aclanthology.org/2020.emnlp-main.140/) - Mehri and Eskenazi. EMNLP. _instrument; human, automatic evaluation._ Fine-grained dimensions such as coherence, error recovery, consistency, and engagingness.
## Automatic Evaluation

- **2002** [BLEU: A method for automatic evaluation of machine translation](https://aclanthology.org/P02-1040/) - Papineni, Roukos, Ward, Zhu. ACL. _metric; automatic evaluation._ Not dialog-specific, but became a common response-generation baseline metric later criticized for weak dialog validity.
- **2004** [Effects of speech recognition accuracy on performance of DARPA Communicator spoken dialogue systems](https://www.nist.gov/publications/effects-speech-recognition-accuracy-performance-darpa-communicator-spoken-dialogue) - Sanders and Le. NIST. _analysis; automatic, human evaluation._ Empirical link between WER, task completion, and satisfaction in travel-planning dialog systems.
- **2015** [The evaluation of conversational agents: Metrics and measures](https://arxiv.org/abs/1704.04579) - Radziwill and Benton. arXiv. _method; automatic evaluation._ Broad evaluation taxonomy spanning efficiency, effectiveness, satisfaction, and conversational quality.
- **2016** [How NOT to evaluate your dialogue system: An empirical study of unsupervised evaluation metrics](https://aclanthology.org/D16-1230/) - Liu, Lowe, Serban, Noseworthy, Charlin, Pineau. EMNLP. _critique; automatic, human evaluation._ Shows many word-overlap metrics correlate weakly with human judgments for open-domain dialog.
- **2020** [BERTScore: Evaluating text generation with BERT](https://openreview.net/forum?id=SkeHuCVFDr) - Zhang, Kishore, Wu, Weinberger, Artzi. ICLR. _metric; automatic evaluation._ Embedding-based generation metric often used as a stronger baseline than n-gram overlap for dialog responses.
- **2020** [USR: An unsupervised and reference-free evaluation metric for dialog generation](https://aclanthology.org/2020.acl-main.64/) - Mehri and Eskenazi. ACL. _method; automatic, human evaluation._ Learns reference-free signals for dialog response quality and correlates with human judgments.
- **2021** [QuestEval: Summarization asks for fact-based evaluation](https://aclanthology.org/2021.emnlp-main.529/) - Scialom et al.. EMNLP. _method; automatic evaluation._ QA-based factuality metrics influenced reference-light evaluation designs for generated responses.
- **2022** [Evaluating dialogue generation: A survey of metrics, datasets, and open problems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.06934) - Dialog evaluation survey authors. dialog-generation evaluation literature. _survey; automatic, human evaluation._ Captures the move from turn-level reference matching toward conversation-level and human-aligned metrics.
- **2023** [A survey on evaluation of large language models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03109) - Chang et al.. ACM TIST / arXiv. _survey; automatic, human evaluation._ Relevant for LLM-based dialog systems: capability, alignment, robustness, safety, and human preference evaluation.
## Evaluation Frameworks

- **1991** [DARPA ATIS common evaluation paradigm](https://aclanthology.org/H91-1023/) - Doddington and DARPA SLS community. DARPA Speech and Natural Language Workshop. _benchmark; automatic evaluation._ Standardized answer correctness and common test sets for spoken language systems.
- **1997** [PARADISE: A framework for evaluating spoken dialogue agents](https://doi.org/10.3115/979617.979652) - Walker, Litman, Kamm, Abella. ACL. _framework; human, automatic evaluation._ Unifies task success, dialog costs, and user satisfaction in a predictive performance model.
- **1998** [Evaluating spoken dialogue agents with PARADISE: Two case studies](https://doi.org/10.1006/csla.1998.0110) - Walker, Litman, Kamm, Abella. Computer Speech & Language. _framework; human, automatic evaluation._ Shows how PARADISE can compare agents and predict future performance.
- **2001** [Quantitative and qualitative evaluation of DARPA Communicator spoken dialogue systems](https://aclanthology.org/P01-1066/) - Walker, Passonneau, Boland. ACL. _benchmark; human, automatic evaluation._ Multi-site travel-planning evaluation combining task completion, user surveys, and logged interaction features.
- **2006** [The PARADISE evaluation framework: Issues and findings](https://doi.org/10.1162/coli.2006.32.2.263) - Hajdinjak and Mihelic. Computational Linguistics. _critique; human, automatic evaluation._ Important critique on parameter selection, normalization, ASR-error effects, and satisfaction targets.
- **2017** [A survey of available corpora for building data-driven dialogue systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.05742) - Serban et al.. Dialogue & Discourse. _survey; human, automatic evaluation._ Maps available datasets and benchmark assumptions that shape automatic dialog evaluation.
- **2020** [Survey on evaluation methods for dialogue systems](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10462-020-09866-x) - Deriu, Rodrigo, Otegi, Echegoyen, Rosset, Agirre, Cieliebak. Artificial Intelligence Review. _survey; human, automatic evaluation._ Comprehensive evaluation survey across task-oriented, conversational, and QA dialog systems.
## User Simulation

- **2007** [Agenda-based user simulation for bootstrapping a POMDP dialogue system](https://aclanthology.org/N07-2033/) - Schatzmann, Thomson, Weilhammer, Ye, Young. NAACL-HLT. _method; automatic evaluation._ Widely used user-simulation method for training and evaluating statistical dialog policies.
- **2012** [A survey on metrics for the evaluation of user simulations](https://doi.org/10.1017/S0269888912000343) - Pietquin and Hastie. Knowledge Engineering Review. _survey; automatic evaluation._ Defines why simulator quality matters for reinforcement learning and automated SDS evaluation.
## Datasets and Shared Tasks

- **1990** [The ATIS spoken language systems pilot corpus](https://doi.org/10.3115/116580.116613) - Hemphill, Godfrey, Doddington. DARPA Speech and Natural Language Workshop. _dataset; automatic evaluation._ Air-travel information corpus that anchored early spoken language understanding and database-query evaluation.
- **2013** [The Dialog State Tracking Challenge](https://aclanthology.org/W13-4065/) - Williams, Raux, Ramachandran, Black. SIGDIAL. _benchmark; automatic evaluation._ Introduced shared evaluation for tracking user goals under ASR/SLU uncertainty.
- **2013** [The Let's Go bus information system: A production-grade spoken dialog corpus](https://dialrc.org/letsgo/) - Raux, Bohus, Langner, Black, Eskenazi. CMU / dialog-system literature. _corpus; human, automatic evaluation._ Real deployed bus-information dialogs; important for ecological evaluation and user behavior under noise.
- **2014** [The Second Dialog State Tracking Challenge](https://aclanthology.org/W14-4337/) - Henderson, Thomson, Williams. SIGDIAL. _benchmark; automatic evaluation._ Restaurant-domain state tracking benchmark; consolidated joint-goal accuracy as a core automatic metric.
- **2018** [MultiWOZ: A large-scale multi-domain Wizard-of-Oz dataset for task-oriented dialogue modelling](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.00278) - Budzianowski et al.. EMNLP. _dataset; automatic evaluation._ Defines multi-domain task-oriented benchmarks with inform, success, BLEU, and combined score conventions.
- **2018** [The Second Conversational Intelligence Challenge (ConvAI2)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1902.00098) - Dinan et al.. NeurIPS Competition Track. _benchmark; human, automatic evaluation._ Open-domain persona chat challenge mixing automatic metrics with human pairwise judgments.
- **2019** [Towards scalable multi-domain conversational agents: The Schema-Guided Dialogue Dataset](https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.05855) - Rastogi, Zang, Sunkara, Gupta, Khaitan. AAAI. _benchmark; automatic evaluation._ Schema-guided evaluation for generalizing across services, domains, intents, and slots.
- **2022** [MultiWOZ 2.4: Annotation corrections to improve state tracking evaluation](https://aclanthology.org/2022.sigdial-1.34/) - Ye, Manotumruksa, Yilmaz. SIGDIAL. _dataset; automatic evaluation._ Shows benchmark validity depends on annotation quality; improves test and validation state labels.
- **2023** [Multi3WOZ: A multilingual, multi-domain, multi-parallel dataset for culturally adapted task-oriented dialog systems](https://doi.org/10.1162/tacl_a_00609) - Hu et al.. TACL. _dataset; automatic, human evaluation._ Extends ToD evaluation to multilingual and culturally adapted dialog behavior.
