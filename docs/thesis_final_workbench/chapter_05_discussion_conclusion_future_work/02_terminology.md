# Chapter 5: Discussion, Conclusion, and Future Work - Terminology

## defensible inference

- Definition: claim supported by evidence, denominator, and validity boundary

## descriptive association

- Definition: observed relation without full causal control

## causal claim

- Definition: claim requiring controlled ablation or matched isolation

## future work

- Definition: bounded extension directly tied to a limitation

## Use Rule

- Define a term when it first becomes necessary for the argument.
- Do not introduce terms only because they appear in the software.
